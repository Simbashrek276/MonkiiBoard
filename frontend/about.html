Our Story

MonkiiBoard was born from a simple frustration: why are most mechanical keyboards so cluttered and over-engineered?

Founded in 2023 by a team of designers and engineers who believed that the best tools should be invisible, we set out to create keyboards that enhance productivity without demanding attention.

Today, MonkiiBoard serves minimalists, professionals, and creators worldwide who value quality, simplicity, and thoughtful design.
