Our Story

From basement tinkering to industry innovation

2024

The Spark

Initial research and discovery phase. Explored the mechanical keyboard market, identified gaps in customization, and developed early concepts for innovative PCB designs and keyboard matrix architectures.

Aug 2025

Founding MonkiiBoard

Officially launched MonkiiBoard as a startup. Assembled our core team and established our mission to revolutionize custom keyboard design and manufacturing.

Oct-Nov 2025

Matrix Development

Developed our initial keyboard matrix designs and consolidated all research findings. Refined our product vision and technical approach based on community feedback and market analysis.

Dec 2025

Execution Phase

Moved from planning to production. Building prototypes, finalizing PCB designs, and preparing for our first product launch with cutting-edge features and innovative engineering.
