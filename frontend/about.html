Our Story

From basement tinkering to industry innovation

2019

The Beginning

Started as a weekend project by two engineering students frustrated with the lack of truly customizable keyboards.

2020

First Prototype

Created our first custom PCB design with hot-swappable switches and revolutionary matrix layout.

2021

Community Growth

Launched our online community and began sharing open-source designs with fellow keyboard enthusiasts.

2022

Going Professional

Established MonkiiBoard as a company and launched our first commercial product line.

2023

Innovation Breakthrough

Developed breakthrough wireless technology and advanced switch matrix designs.

2024

Global Expansion

Expanded worldwide and became a trusted name in the mechanical keyboard community.
