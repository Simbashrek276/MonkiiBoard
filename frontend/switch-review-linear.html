Linear Switches Review

Smooth & Silent - Perfect for gaming and fast typing

Overview

Linear switches are characterized by their smooth, consistent keystroke from top to bottom with no tactile bump or click. They're the go-to choice for gamers and typists who prefer a fast, fluid typing experience without distraction.

Technical Specifications

Actuation Force

45-65g

Travel Distance

2.0mm actuation / 4.0mm total

Sound Level

Quiet (2/5)

Lifespan

50-100 million presses

Performance Analysis

Gaming Performance

Linear switches excel in gaming scenarios due to their consistent actuation and quick reset:

  • Fast double-tapping and rapid key presses
  • No tactile bump means smooth transitions
  • Lighter variants (45g) reduce finger fatigue during long sessions
  • Predictable actuation point for precise inputs
  • Quiet operation ideal for streaming and recording
üí° Pro Tip: For competitive FPS gaming, opt for lighter linear switches (45-50g) for faster response times.

Typing Experience

While optimized for gaming, linear switches can also be excellent for typing:

  • Smooth keystrokes reduce finger strain during long writing sessions
  • Heavier variants (60-65g) provide more control and reduce accidental presses
  • Quiet operation suitable for office environments
  • Consistent feel helps develop muscle memory
  • Fast reset enables quick typing speeds

Sound Profile

Linear switches produce minimal sound, making them ideal for shared spaces:

  • No click mechanism means quieter operation
  • Main sound comes from bottoming out
  • Can be further silenced with O-rings or silent variants
  • Lubrication dramatically reduces spring ping

Modding & Optimization

Lubrication

Apply thin lube (Krytox 205g0) to rails and spring for ultra-smooth operation. Avoid over-lubing which can make switches mushy.

Spring Swapping

Replace stock springs with custom weights to fine-tune actuation force. Popular choices: 62g, 67g, or progressive springs.

Filming

Add switch films to reduce housing wobble and tighten tolerances for improved sound and feel.

Breaking In

New linear switches benefit from a break-in period. Type on them for a few days or actuate them 1000+ times for optimal smoothness.

Our Verdict

Linear switches are the best choice for users who prioritize smoothness, speed, and quiet operation. They're particularly well-suited for gaming where consistent actuation and rapid key presses are essential.
