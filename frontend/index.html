Frequently Asked Questions

What makes MonkiiBoard keyboards different?

Our keyboards are handcrafted with premium components, custom PCB designs, and meticulous attention to detail. Each build is optimized for performance, durability, and the perfect typing experience.

Do you offer custom keyboard builds?

Yes! We specialize in custom builds tailored to your exact specifications. Choose your layout, switches, keycaps, and case design. Contact us to start your custom project.

What is the typical build time?

Standard builds typically take 2-3 weeks. Custom builds may take 4-6 weeks depending on component availability and customization requirements. We'll keep you updated throughout the process.

What warranty do you provide?

All MonkiiBoard keyboards come with a 1-year warranty covering manufacturing defects. We also offer lifetime support for troubleshooting and maintenance guidance.
