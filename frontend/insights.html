PCB Technology

Hot-Swap Technology

Our PCBs feature advanced hot-swappable switch sockets, allowing users to change mechanical switches without soldering. This modular approach enables endless customization possibilities.

  • • Kailh Hot-swap sockets for 3-pin and 5-pin switches
  • • Gold-plated contacts for optimal conductivity
  • • Rated for 100+ switch changes per socket
  • • Compatible with MX-style switches

RGB Integration

Subtle RGB underglow provides ambient lighting without compromising the minimal aesthetic. Our custom lighting algorithms create smooth, eye-pleasing effects.

  • • 16.7 million color possibilities
  • • Per-key RGB customization
  • • Low power consumption design
  • • Software-free lighting modes
